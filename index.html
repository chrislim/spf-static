Big companies like Google and Microsoft are throwing lots of money and PhDs at the problem of fully automatic speech translation. The quality of their results is good enough for alot of settings, but not for delivering a quality presentation. We focus on helping you craft a great experience so people will want to know what you have to say. All you need to do is prepare.

How it works

Doing anything well requires preparation and this is especially true when serving a mutlilingual audience.

We designed SPF.IO to be straightforward to use. You craft your message. Someone translates it. You deliver your message, with subtitles and slides appearing on a screen in sync with your speech. Your audience follows in their native language on their mobile devices. They can also take notes or give you feedback. Afterwards, you can review what they said and address it.

Humans are needed to craft the message, translate it, and show subtitles at the right time. SPF has automatic tools to assist with each step, but it cannot replace people yet.

This simple process has been used on a weekly basis at a bilingual church for over a year. It enables the congregation to stay together instead of being segregated by language. You can increase the reach of your message by using SPF.IO too.

Give the gift of literacy and intercultural understanding

We're passionate about worldwide literacy and giving a voice to people who speak minority languages. What's the point of enabling awesome presentations if most people in the world can't enjoy it? What's the point if we only help people who are already resource rich and fail to give a voice to those who can only express themselves in their native language?

SPF is designed to do both of these things. We make it easy for you to get your content into many langauges and share it widely. We also make it possible for people from other languages to create and share their content in many languages. Here is how it works.

When you use SPF and submit translated content, this information is collected and used to improve a machine translation system. The more data the system has, the better it performs so that translations become easier and cheaper to produce the more content you add.

Secondly, when you financially support SPF by giving or paying for plan, a portion of the funds is set aside to subsidize the cost of the platform for people all around the world. As they create more content, they also improve the machine translation system for their languages.

At the heart of all of this is of course great content. It doesn't matter what language is used, when there is great content, people want it--language is just one thing that prevents them from enjoying it.

We believe that by making it easy to produce, share and enjoy multilingual content from in-person experiences, we will give a voice to those who have none, advance worldwide literacy and help people simply get to know each other and understand each other from their unique cultural experiences.
